Field Support Resources

These materials are designed to help ordinary people handle difficult real-life human situations more safely and calmly.

They are especially used when someone notices a person who may be overwhelmed, withdrawn, distressed, or struggling â€” but does not know how to approach them without making things worse.

The goal is not counseling and not intervention.

The goal is:

  • noticing early signs
  • responding gently
  • avoiding escalation
  • knowing when to step back
  • helping without pressure or harm

They function as a small guided rehearsal for real-world moments that families, educators, peer supporters, and community members regularly encounter.

These resources are shared for educational and supportive purposes only. They are not therapy, crisis services, or professional advice.

If someone may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency or crisis services.
